Frequently Asked Questions

What is ISO 15939?

ISO/IEC 15939 is an international standard that defines a measurement process applicable to system and software engineering and management disciplines. It provides a framework for collecting, analyzing, and using measurement data.

How do I use the Quality Assessment Simulator?

Navigate to the "Simulator" page and follow the 4-step process: Select a case study, assign weights to quality dimensions, input metric values, and view the analysis results. The tool guides you through each step.

Can I create my own measurement scenario?

Yes, in the Simulator, you can choose "Custom Scenario" to select specific quality dimensions that are relevant to your project, instead of using a pre-defined case study.

What do the charts show?

The radar chart in the analysis section visualizes the performance of different quality dimensions against their targets. It helps identify areas that meet requirements and those that need improvement.

How are the scores calculated?

Scores are calculated based on the weighted average of the input values for each dimension. The weights reflect the importance of each dimension in your specific context.
